I am an assistant professor of the Institute of Public Service at Seattle University. I got my PhD from the School of Public Affairs and Administration (SPAA) at Rutgers University-Newark. My researches topics of public administration and nonprofit management, including the sector stereotype in social service market, inter-sectoral collaboration and competition.
